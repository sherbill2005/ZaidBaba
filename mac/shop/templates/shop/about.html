ZaidBaba - About Us

My Awesome Cart is an online retail service based in Pakistan and owned by the Group. Launched in 2022, it is made up of small businesses in Pakistan and other locations, such as Singapore, that offer products to international online buyers. It is the most visited e-commerce website in the world and was the 10th most popular website in Brazil. It facilitates small businesses to sell to customers all over the world. My Awesome Cart has drawn comparison to eBay, as sellers are independent and use the platform to offer products to buyers.

Business model

Sellers on My Awesome Cart can be either companies or individuals. AliExpress is different from Amazon because it acts only as an e-commerce platform and does not itself sell products directly to consumers. It directly connects Chinese businesses with buyers.

Awards

Zaid Ahmed was awarded Entrepreneur of the Year, 2022 from The Economic Times, a leading Indian economic daily newspaper. In September 2022, the two founders entered Forbes India's richest Indian by year, debuting in the 86th position with a net worth of US$1.3 billion each. In April 2022, Zaid Ahmed was named to Time magazine's annual list of the 100 Most Influential People in the World.
